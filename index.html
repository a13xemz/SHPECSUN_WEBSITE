recent events

August 5-9, 2015

29th Annual National Institute for Leadership Advancement (NILA)

The 29th annual National Institute for Leadership Advancement conference was hosted at Facebook Headquarters in Menlo Park,CA. NILA is SHPE's premier leadership training program for the upcoming years executive board members. This conference offers:

  • Experience hands on activities that will strengthen your soft skills and your chapter.
  • Engage and interact with over 200 of the brightest Latinos and Hispanic professionals, students, and corporate representatives from Fortune 500 Companies.
  • Evolve as leaders through case study projects that will challenge and strengthen your leadership skills.

    • This conference showed me the true importance of being a great leader in my chapter. Everything I learned through workshops and guest speakers will definitely be relayed to my members to allow them to be leaders in their communities, families and classrooms. I also had the chance to network with leaders from chapters all over the nation and you learn what their strengths and weaknesses were so we can grow as a national organization and progress towards empowering Hispanics in STEM fields. I am truly grateful for this experience as I have learned great skills, networked with the future STEM Leaders of the world, and connected with my SHPE FAMILIA.
    - Christopher Zendejas
